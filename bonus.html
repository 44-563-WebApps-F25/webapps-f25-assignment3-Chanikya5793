Iron Man & Spider-Man

Armor and agility united for smart heroics.

Team Overview

Iron Man brings adaptive powered armor, strategic targeting systems, and resource orchestration. Spider-Man contributes spider-sense precognition, acrobatic mobility, and web-based battlefield control. Together they form a rapid-response partnership blending engineering scale with improvisational finesse.

Powers & Abilities

  • Iron Man: Modular nano-armor deployment, multi-spectral HUD, repulsor & unibeam arrays.
  • Spider-Man: Spider-sense danger detection, enhanced agility & strength, web fluid tech versatility.

Primary Foes

  • Doctor Octopus – AI-augmented mechanical arms enabling multi-vector assaults.
  • Mandarin – Ten rings channeling diverse energy signatures & elemental effects.

Support & Tools

  • Shared comms encryption bridging Stark satellites & Spider-tracer mesh.
  • Hybrid suit calibration sessions for joint maneuver drills.
